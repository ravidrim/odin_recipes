Tofu with Soy Sauce

A savory way of enjoying a asian staple

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions, chopped fine
  • Gochu-garu ( Red chilli pepper flakes)
  • Garlic, mashed

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into slabs
  2. Mix the green onions with the garlic and the Soy Sauce in a seperate saucer
  3. Cook tofu on a pan on high heat, flip once golden on one side
  4. Spoon over the sauce onto the cooked, golden side of the tofu, flip again once the other side is golden
  5. Flip a final time, and cook through, making sure the sauce isn't burning in the process
  6. Serve hot with rice
