Curry

A simple method of making Curry with rice, with plenty of vegetables and chicken inside

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Chicken breast
  • Carrots, medium dice
  • Onions, medium dice
  • Curry powder, 125ml

Steps

  1. Prepare rice
  2. Chop vegetables and protein according to size
  3. Saute vegetables and chicken with vegetable oil, until vegetables are translucent and the meat well-browned
  4. Temove from pan, and add curry powder and more oil, if need be
  5. Cook out the powder just before it browns, add stock/water in parts to create volume
  6. Add vegetables back in once sauce is of enough size and consistency, and stir/mix well
  7. Serve sauce with steaming rice
