Korean-Chinese Fried Rice

Quick, no-frills way of making a comfort staple in Korean cuisuine

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Carrots, small dice
  • Onions, small dice
  • 2 eggs, beaten very well

Steps

  1. Prepare rice
  2. Cut both vegetables into appropriate size
  3. Saute vegetables on a pan in medium amounts of vegetable oil
  4. Add rice once vegetables are translucent, cook until rice developes a oily shine, adding more oil if mixture seemes dry
  5. Add the beaten egg mixture evenly over the rice, and toss mixture vigorously, in order to incorporate the egg evenly to the rice
  6. Season to taste. Serve hot
